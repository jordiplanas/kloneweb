PHOTOGRAMMETRY

Photogrammetry is a technique that consists in taking thousands of pictures of and object to automatically create a digital 3D model in the most realistic, fastest and versatile way.

QUALITY

By taking more than 100 photographs at maximum resolution of the product to be scanned, a photorealistic texture is obtained. This texture is impossible to recreate with 3D techniques since the level of detail, colors and finishes of the models are the most faithful to the real product.

SPEED

With the multiple camera system and automated scanning software, only one shot is needed. Then the program begins to rebuild the model and in a matter of hours it can be ready to send to the client. Therefore it also supports rapid scanning of numerous product collections.

REUSABILITY

Once a single shot has been made, the resulting 3D model can be used in multiple digital environments. Videos, campaign images, apps, websites ... Since you can digitally modify texture, shapes and animations our models are ready to create any imagined content.

EFFICENCY

For scanning 3D models only a photographer and a 3D artist are required. This avoids costly productions, large equipment, travel, etc.On the other hand, modeling in 3D using traditional techniques takes much more time without achieving the photorealistic texture of photogrammetry.
